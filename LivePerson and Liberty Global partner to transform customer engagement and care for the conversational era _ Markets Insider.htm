LivePerson Brings a Porsche Engine to the Mid Market

PRESS RELEASE PR Newswire
June. 18, 2018, 06:00 AM







LONDON, March 22, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- LivePerson, Inc., (Nasdaq: LPSN) the leading provider of cloud mobile and online business messaging solutions, adjusts its enterprise messaging eco system, making is suitable for small businesses. The company wraps all the heavy logic, high scale system into a lite weight package that can serve the small and medium size market.

Liveperson takes 3 steps towards the SMB market adjusting the onboarding time, payment handling and AI for small businesses needs.

Ultra Fast Onboarding

The company shortens the onboarding time to seconds, being up and running in no time. New customers are drawn to embed a special link that invites users to start a conversation.

Onboarding contain only:
Email and password
In return - the customer gets a link to initiate a messaging session.
More complex integrations are available but the user is up and running in seconds No campaigns, No skills, No customer service Jargon

Adjusted pricing model

The company integrated the top merchant payment services, making it easy to access premium features without calling anyone, contacting a salesperson, or inserting a credit card number. Upgrade your account with paypal and blueSnap in minutes.

Small business owners are able to sense the simplicity and benefits of the product using a full featured trial period. When the trial period is over, there are 2 simple upgrade flows

  • Extra seats per month
  • Premium functionality for a fixed cost


Advisory bot

A bot that uses AI + predefined information to either solve immediate issues or connect the human agent. Liveperson incorporates AI and predefined content inserted by the customer to create a custom bot for each business. The bots immidialy take the conversation, try to identify simple intents and use the business information to answer. If the bot is having trouble it’ll explain:
“I’m connecting you with the store manager, he’s the real expert”
